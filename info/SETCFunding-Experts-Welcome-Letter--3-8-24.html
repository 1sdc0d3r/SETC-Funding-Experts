How Do I Apply for SETC?


If you haven’t already applied, use our Pre-Qualification system at  https://setcpros.com/setcfundingexperts/.  Select the link “Start Application to begin qualifying.


The process is quite simple. It boils down to the four simple steps below and only takes 8-10 minutes:


4 Steps to Qualifying:

  • Get Qualified – Begin your application and confirm your eligibility by answering the pre-qualifying questions at https://setcpros.com/setcfundingexperts/ (We recommend using your cell phone for the application process).
  • Submit Document Request – Complete request to verify your financial information with the IRS through our secure portal.
  • File Documents – Once approved, you will receive the filing documents from us, which require a “wet” or actual signature along with pre-paid mailing label and instructions. 
  • Receive Refund – You’ll receive your refund in 4-16 weeks.


Are You Eligible?


SETC applies to self-employed individuals who were unable to work or telework due to:

  • Federal, state, or local quarantine or isolation due to covid 19, or
  • Being advised by a healthcare provider to self-quarantine due to covid, or
  • Experiencing covid-related symptoms and seeking a medical diagnosis, or
  • Caring for an individual due to covid (i.e. children, grandkids, family member etc.), or
  • Caring for a child if their school is closed or childcare provider is unavailable due to covid.
